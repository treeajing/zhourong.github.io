Research Experience

  • Elaborate Bladder Cancer Knowledge Graph for Question Answering System Sept. 2020 - Aug. 2021

    Abstract: We constructed an elaborate Bladder Cancer Knowledge Graph (BCKG) with a novelty human-machine construction method containing a hierarchical knowledge model, and implemented a question answering system based on BCKG. Firstly, we designed a hierarchical model to provide an architectural framework and knowledge base for the subsequent knowledge graph construction. Then we trained a joint entity recognition (NER) and relation extraction (RE) model based on BERT-BiLSTM-CRF framework for Chinese medical text (clinical guidelines in this study) to extract knowledge. Thirdly, we constructed the BCKG based on the hierarchical model and knowledge extracted by the joint model. Lastly, we implemented a question answering system that provides self-help access to medical knowledge about bladder cancer.
    My Contribution
    • Designed the hierarchical knowledge model
    • Trained the joint NER and RE model and extract knowledge from clinical guidelines
    • Implemented a question answering system

  • Multimodal Computer-assisted Diagnosis of Bladder Cancer Dec. 2016 - Dec. 2017

    Abstract: We proposed a multimodal feature space from CT images and pathological whole slice images (WSI) , and created a deep learning model to help diagnose bladder cancer. Firstly, we proposed a multimodal feature space containing radiological features and pathological features, with multimodal associations analysis of CT and WSI performed. Then we created a convolutional neural network to predict the molecular typing of bladder cancer by multimodal feature fusion.
    My Contribution
    • Trained the convolutional neural network

  • The Design and Implementation of Chinese-Uighur-English Online Dictionary and Semantic Search Based on Knowledge Graph Sept. 2020 - Aug. 2021

    Abstract: We constructed a Uighur Knowledge Graph(UKG) and implemented a Chinese-Uighur-English online dictionary and semantic search based on the UKG for ethnic scientific research and education. Firstly, we proposed a K-means clustering algorithm for the similarity classification of Uighur vocabulary. Then we created a UKG based on the word-formation characteristics of Uighur vocabulary. Lastly, we employed the UKG to design a Chinese-Uyghur-English online dictionary with a huge thesaurus and to develop a semantic search engine that provides associative information query results with real-time translation among Chinese Uyghur and English.
    My Contribution
