Research Experience

  • Elaborate Bladder Cancer Knowledge Graph for Question Answering System Sep. 2020 - Aug. 2021

    Abstract: We constructed an elaborate Bladder Cancer Knowledge Graph (BCKG) with a novelty human-machine construction method containing a hierarchical knowledge model, and implemented a question answering system based on BCKG. Firstly, we designed a hierarchical model to provide an architectural framework and knowledge base for the subsequent knowledge graph construction. Then we trained a joint entity recognition (NER) and relation extraction (RE) model based on BERT-BiLSTM-CRF framework for Chinese medical text (clinical guidelines in this study) to extract knowledge. Thirdly, we constructed the BCKG based on the hierarchical model and knowledge extracted by the joint model. Lastly, we implemented a question answering system that provides self-help access to medical knowledge about bladder cancer.
